## What this project is

Escuela Brahmavidya is a spiritual learning platform built around a structured method for personal transformation. The method guides users through a series of milestones — each requiring written reflection — culminating in actualization into the practice. The platform also hosts video courses available via donation (not a subscription paywall — users donate to access content).

The prototype is live at the project URL (Roger will share the link). Open it in any browser on your phone or computer — no setup required. Navigate through it as a new visitor would.

## Design milestones

### Milestone 1 — Landing page ✅ COMPLETE

The public-facing marketing page. Navigate this first. Contains:

1. **Floating pill navbar** — sticky, frosted glass, "Begin" CTA
2. **Hero section** — headline, two CTAs, preview card showing path progress
3. **Path to Enlightenment** — 6-step card grid showing the journey in preview (steps 1–2 completed, 3 in progress, 4–6 locked)
4. **Video courses catalog** — bento grid of 5 courses with pricing and thumbnails
5. **Marquee strip** — partner institutions scrolling
6. **Closing band** — dark surface, pricing, "Create your account" CTA
7. **Footer**

---

### Milestone 2 — Sign-up & onboarding flow 🔲 NOT STARTED

Screens needed:
- Account creation form (email, password, basic profile)
- Email verification interstitial
- Welcome / method introduction screen (what the user is entering into, what's expected)
- First-time path orientation (brief explanation of the 3-step structure before they begin)

---

### Milestone 3 — User dashboard 🔲 NOT STARTED

The hub the user returns to after sign-up. Shows:
- Overall path progress (which step they're on, what's completed)
- Progress ring / step indicator (previewed on the hero card in M1 — this is the full version)
- Reflection count per step
- Entry point into the current step
- Access to purchased/donated courses

---

### Milestone 4 — Step experience (×3 steps) 🔲 NOT STARTED

Each of the 3 spiritual milestones has its own experience screen. Design needed for:

- **Step landing** — title, description, what's required to complete this step
- **Reflection prompt** — the question or exercise the user responds to
- **Reflection submission UI** — text input, character guidance, submit action
- **Verification state** — what the user sees when a reflection is received and under review
- **Step completion** — the moment a step is marked complete and the next unlocks

All three steps share the same screen structure but may carry different visual weight or color treatment to signal progression.

---

### Milestone 5 — Actualization state 🔲 NOT STARTED

The end state when a user completes all steps. This is a significant moment in the method.  
Needs: completion visual, summary of the journey, what comes next (community? continued practice? advanced content?).

---

### Milestone 6 — Video course experience 🔲 NOT STARTED

- Course detail page (description, instructor, curriculum outline, donation CTA)
- Donation / access flow (how the user donates and unlocks content)
- Course player (video embed, chapter navigation, progress tracking)

---

### Milestone 7 — Mobile review 🔲 NOT STARTED

All screens above reviewed and adjusted for smartphone layout. Confirm the mobile experience is coherent, not just scaled down.
